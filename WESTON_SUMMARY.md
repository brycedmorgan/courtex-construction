## 🎨 **"Kinetic Precision" Design System**

We developed a sophisticated design framework that positions CourTex as the **"Nike Lab of court construction"** - premium, performance-focused, and championship-quality.

### Core Design Principles:
- **High-End Editorial Aesthetic** (Vogue meets Nike Lab)
- **Action Green (#cbf749)** - High-impact accent used strategically
- **Obsidian-green black (#05170c)** - Rich surface color with depth
- **Space Grotesk + Inter** - Athletic precision typography
- **Intentional Asymmetry** - Breaking template conventions
- **"No-line rule"** - Tonal shifts instead of 1px borders

- **11ty Static Site Generator** - Blazing fast load times
- **Schema Markup** - Organization, FAQ, and breadcrumb schemas
- **Dynamic Sitemap** - Auto-generated from content
- **brand-facts.json** - Machine-readable data for LLM crawlers
- **Mobile-first responsive** - Perfect on all devices
- **WCAG 2.1 Compliant** - Full accessibility standards

### Interactive Effects (All 11 Implemented):
1. **Rotating gradient hero** - Brand color cycling animation
2. **Gradient shimmer buttons** - Light sweep on CTAs
3. **Hover lift cards** - translateY + shadow + border animations
4. **Image zoom breaks** - Slow scale transforms
5. **Scroll fade-in** - IntersectionObserver with stagger delays
6. **Partner logo effects** - Grayscale to color transitions
7. **Nav scroll shadow** - Appears on scroll
8. **Step number pulse** - Glowing process steps
9. **FAQ accordion** - Native `<details>` with rotating icons
10. **Mobile nav toggle** - Smooth slide animation
11. **Video hero background** - Auto-playing court footage
